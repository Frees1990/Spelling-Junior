HOW TO PLAY GAME

PLAYER ENTRY

  1. NAME ====> "Please enter your Player Name
  2. NUMBER OF WORDS ====> "Enter the number of words you want to spell"
  3. START GAME ====> "Press to ENTER GAME ROOM"

GAME BOARD

  1. Press "NEW WORD" to Generate word
  2. Once word has been generated, input your answer to the "INPUT BOX"
  3. Click the the "CHECK WORD"
  4. If you want to hear the word again click "REPEAT"
"Alert box will pop at the top of the page for further info and instructions"
